Mom's Chili

Description

Nothing says the weather is getting cooler like a nice bowl of chili. This recipe has a bit of a kick to it with the addition of jalapenos and goes perfect with some homemade corn bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Ground Beef
  • 1 lb Cubed Steak
  • 2 Cans Dark Kidney Beans
  • 2 Cans Light Kidney Beans
  • 2 Cans Diced Tomatoes
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 4 Jalapenos

Instructions

  1. Empty the cans of dark and light kidney beans into a slow cooker, draining the liquid from one can of each.
  2. Empty the can of diced tomatoes into the slow cooker also draining the liquid from one can.
  3. Season the slow cooker with the chili powder, cumin, salt and pepper.
  4. Brown the ground beef and add to the slow cooker after draining the fat and grease.
  5. Brown the steak and add to the slow cooker.
  6. Slice the jalapenos into small rings and add to the slow cooker.
  7. Cook on low for 6 hours.
